Welcome to the NBA Expansion Draft Simulator

The NBA is expected to add two new teams (Seattle & Las Vegas) as early as the 2028-29 season. This simulator uses 2025-2026 real rosters and contracts to let you run the expansion draft as if it happened right now.

1

Protection Phase

Each of the 30 existing teams protects up to 8 players. Protected players are safe and cannot be selected by the expansion teams.

2

Expansion Draft

Seattle and Las Vegas take turns picking players from the unprotected pool. They can only take 1 player per team.
